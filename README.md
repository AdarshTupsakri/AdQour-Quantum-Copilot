# AdQour Quantum Copilot

An AI-powered platform that helps beginners build, understand, debug, optimize, and learn quantum circuits.

## Vision

Quantum computing tools are powerful, but most assume users already understand quantum mechanics, circuit design, and quantum algorithms.

AdQour Quantum Copilot aims to bridge this gap by acting as an intelligent learning and development assistant for quantum computing.

Instead of only showing outputs and state vectors, the platform helps users understand:

* What a circuit does
* Why it works
* How it can be optimized
* Where it is used in the real world

---

## Problem Statement

Current quantum platforms focus on simulation and execution.

Beginners often struggle with:

* Understanding quantum gates
* Interpreting state vectors
* Identifying circuit purpose
* Learning optimization techniques
* Connecting theory to practical applications

AdQour Quantum Copilot is designed to make quantum circuits understandable and accessible.

---

## MVP Features

### Circuit Builder

Create quantum circuits using a visual interface.

### Quantum Simulation

Simulate circuits using Qiskit.

### AI Circuit Explanation

Receive step-by-step explanations of how a circuit works.

### Reverse Engineering

Analyze existing circuits and identify their purpose.

### Circuit Optimization

Detect redundant gates and suggest simplifications.

### Quantum Mistake Detection

Identify common beginner mistakes and explain how to fix them.

### State Evolution Visualization

Visualize how quantum states change throughout circuit execution.
